Outsourcing: Your All-in-One Creative Partner

At Shapers, we’re not just a production house—we’re a resource and a partner for other creative companies.

If you’re a production or post-production team looking for extra hands, we’ve got you covered.

We offer everything from on-site shooting and editing to specialized post-production services like color grading, sound design, translation, and more.

Whether you need us to jump in and help you finish a project on a tight timeline, provide a crew member to handle live sound or filming, or just lend our expertise in any part of the process, we’re here to support you.

Think of us as an extension of your own team. We bring the same dedication and quality to your project as we do to our own, ensuring everything is delivered at the highest standard.

You handle the big picture, we handle the details—giving you back your time and sanity.
