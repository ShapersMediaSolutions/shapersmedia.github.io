About

Who Are We?

Shapers Media Solutions

We’re a Kitchener–Waterloo–based media studio dedicated to amplifying artists, cultural organizations, and creative communities. We specialize in cinematic, documentary-style storytelling — showing not just what artists create, but why they create.

Our work bridges art and audience through authenticity, empathy, and design. From behind-the-scenes films and live performance features to archival video restoration and branded artist reels, we capture the deeper story that defines each creative journey.

Beyond production, Shapers is a creative hub for collaboration and growth. We provide training, mentorship, and paid opportunities for newcomers and emerging artists to gain hands-on experience in video, sound, and storytelling — shaping a diverse, collaborative, and deeply human creative landscape.
