Rama Barbaros

Rama Samkari is a creative generalist and storyteller in Istanbul whose work spans illustration, animation, art direction, and concept consulting.

She collaborates with organizations, particularly in education, to make projects more enjoyable, interactive, and accessible, drawing on a background in media and multidisciplinary design.

Her portfolio highlights a strong emphasis on kids’ content and narrative-led visuals, alongside broader brand and concept development. Samkari has been recognized at Behance’s Istanbul Portfolio Review (First Place) and continues to share work-in-progress and finished pieces on Behance.
